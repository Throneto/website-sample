Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to VALARZAI Knowledge Base Management System (hereinafter referred to as "the System", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information, particularly email data accessed through the Gmail API.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Gmail integration feature of the System, we collect the following information:

  • Email Content: Including email subject, body, sender, recipient, and date
  • Email Metadata: Such as email ID, labels, and categorization information
  • Basic Google Account Information: Necessary information for authentication purposes
Important Notice: We only request Gmail read-only permissions (gmail.readonly). We will not modify, delete, or send any emails on your behalf.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Importing emails you select into your personal knowledge base
  • Automatically categorizing emails based on content (Hobby / Niche)
  • Providing email search and management functionality
  • Improving system features and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the following measures to protect your data security:

  • Encrypted Storage: All data is encrypted in our database
  • Access Control: Only you can access your knowledge base data
  • Secure Transmission: All data is transmitted using HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Minimization Principle: We only store necessary email information
  • Regular Backups: To prevent accidental data loss

5. Google API Services Usage

The System uses the Google Gmail API to access your email data. We comply with the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the "Limited Use" requirements.

Limited Use Disclosure:
  • VALARZAI's use of information received from Gmail APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements
  • We only use data to provide or improve user-facing features
  • We do not transfer data to third parties (unless legally required or with your explicit consent)
  • We do not use data for serving advertisements
  • We do not allow humans to read your data (unless you give explicit consent, or for security purposes)

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

We may only disclose information in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal regulations
  • To protect system security and prevent fraud
  • With your explicit authorization

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access Rights: View imported email data at any time
  • Deletion Rights: Delete any imported emails or your entire knowledge base
  • Revocation Rights: Revoke Gmail access authorization at any time
  • Export Rights: Export all your knowledge base data

8. How to Revoke Authorization

You can revoke the System's access to your Gmail at any time:

  1. Visit Google Account Permissions
  2. Find "VALARZAI" application
  3. Click "Remove Access"

After revocation, we will no longer be able to access your Gmail. However, previously imported data will remain in your knowledge base unless you manually delete it.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The System uses necessary cookies to maintain user login status and session management. We do not use any third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

The System is not directed at children under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from children, we will immediately delete such data.

11. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you through the System. Please review this page periodically to stay informed about our latest privacy practices.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

13. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by deleting your account or specific knowledge items.

14. Third-Party Services

The System integrates with Google Gmail API. Please note that Google's use of your data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for Google's privacy practices.

15. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@valarzai.com
  • Website: https://valarzai.com

Your Consent

By using the System, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our collection and use of information as described herein. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the System.

Compliance

We are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws, including but not limited to:

  • Google API Services User Data Policy
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable
